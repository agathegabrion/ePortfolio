Civic engagement

Check out some of my engagements

In addition to all the technical skills we learn at ENSIACET, we also do many different activities to become responsible and engaged engineers.

La Fresque du Climat

I had the chance to participate in a workshop organised by "La Fresque du Climat". It's a collaborative workshop which aims at building a fresco that explains how the biodiversity is evolving using a set of cards containing causes and consequences of climate change.

Le Procès de King-Kong

Within my first weeks at ENSIACET, I participated in "Le Procès de King-Kong", which is an interactive theater piece played by professionals in order to raise awareness about sexual and sexist violence. After each scene, the main character asks us questions to start a debate about what had just happened. Every participant can answer or ask a question before the next scene starts.

Sulitest

The Sulitest is an online multiple-choice-question test that aims at mainstreaming sustainability literacy worldwide. Within one of my courses at ENSIACET, I was able to pass this test and become more aware of the social, economical and environmental challenges the world is currently facing.

TASK by Sulitest

After discovering and passing the Sulitest, I also participated in the Pilot version of a sustainability lliteracy awareness test called TASK. The goal was to test and assess the accuracy and relevance of its content. The actual test will be launched in spring 2023.

Human Library

I recently volunteered to help setting up a Human Library for the organization AVEC. The concept of a Human library is to replace books with people in order for readers to hear their personal story. This aims at encouraging empathy and understanding.
